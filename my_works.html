Media grupa

Media Group d.o.o. is located in Koprivnica at the address Dravska 17, and operates throughout Croatia and abroad. In addition to the sale of substitute inks and toners, the main activity is the production of promotional materials, sales of office supplies, printing on shirts and sales of printing equipment. They offer everything for company promotion: T-shirts, cups, pendants, tassels, balloons.

Teeshirtie

Teeshirtie lets you design, discover, and sell your own merchandise designs. We understand how important your T-shirt project is to you. That's why we've established exactingstandards for print quality and offer multiple printing technologies to ensure that your tees will come out great.
